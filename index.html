Welcome to aiquizzes!

This used to be a website you could sign up for and learn with spaced repetition.

However, I found it unfeasible to continue to maintain the site, so I have open-sourced the Anki decks that contain the questions and answers from the platform.

If you had been using this site up until the point at which it was archived, please accept my sincerest apologies for any inconvenience this may cause. Over the years, I spent hundreds of dollars on hosting aiquizzes (which was 100% free), but maintaining a site which uses technology I no longer use (rails) has become increasingly difficult.

Thank you for your understanding and I hope the Anki decks will serve as a useful resource for your AI learning journey.
