Question 10/11 fast.ai v4 lecture 3

When blogging, should you think about the most advanced thing you know and try to write a blog post that would impress Geoff Hinton? Why?

Answer

That is generally not a good idea. Most people are not Geoff Hinton. Trying to blog for someone who has more expertise than you is not likely to work well. Another consequence of this approach is that you have a small audience now - there is far more people who are not familiar with deep learning than who are.

The best advice? Try writing something that you from 6 months ago would find super interesting and very useful!
