In the Volkswagen diesel cheating case, who was the first person to end up in prison?

Answer

Not a board member, not the CEO, not the managers who coordinated the efforts - it was an engineer. They were following orders from what their boss told them to do, but that is not a good excuse for doing something that is unethical.
